    Tutorial

    Woodstock will turn your chord progressions into four-part chorales.

    You can enter chord symbols:

      Example: C F D/F# G C/E Dm C7 GMm7 CM7 Cm/Eb

      Roman numerals work too!

        Example: D: I IV V vi I bvi V viidim I V65 #V7 Vaug6 V I
